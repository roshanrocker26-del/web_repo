Our Journey

From a small unit in Chennai to an enterprise empowering schools nationwide. Here is the story of how we started and where we are today.

Our Inception Since Start

2001

The Beginning

Founded as a unit of USS Unique Software Solutions to provide high-quality IT curriculum to local schools in Chennai.

2010

Regional Expansion

Expanded our footprint across South India, reaching over 500 schools and introducing our flagship "I-Smart" series.

2018

Digital Transformation

Introduced QR-coded interactive video lessons, integrating practical demonstrations to bridge the gap between theory and practice.

Present

Pioneering EdTech

Serving 2500+ schools nationwide, constantly innovating with AI, Robotics, and NEP 2020-aligned experiential learning frameworks.
